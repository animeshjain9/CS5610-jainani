About the Experiment

So my final project is mainly oriented around a baby-care website with a small e-commerce system in place. Parents of the infants, babies and toddlers can actually buy and sell baby care items like the ones we usually find in the market - clothes, sanitary items, food products, etc. These products can be brought and sold using an e-commerce cart I wish to develop, just to give a real feel to my website.
